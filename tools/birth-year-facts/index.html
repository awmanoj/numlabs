    What this does

    The highlights are a hand-picked list of what released or happened in well-known years — the films, gadgets, games, and moments people remember. Years outside that list still get the rest of the card.

    The age is simply this year minus your year. The generation uses the common Western cohort bands (Boomer, Gen X, Millennial, Gen Z, and so on). The Chinese zodiac follows the twelve-animal cycle and its five elements, repeating every sixty years. The leap-year check uses the full Gregorian rule, and the Roman numerals are valid for years 1 through 3999.
